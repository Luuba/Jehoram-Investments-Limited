About us

Jehoram Investments Limited is a wholly Zambian owned, managed and registered company based in Livingstone, Zambia. It was founded in June 1997 due to the conditions in the construction and maintenance industry, which was a run down as result of; 1. Zambia reliance on foreign expertise 2. Low return on investments in real estate 3. Zambias liberalize economy

Business / Service

1. Civil Construction;
  • Construction of residential, commercial & Industrial buildings.

  • Water, Drainage & Sewerage infrastructure.
